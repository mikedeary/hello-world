ABOUT US

JK Rendering Systems are a proud professionally run business with over 20 combined years experience.

We provide the ultimate service

JK Rendering Systems is a external rendering company based in Southport, Merseyside offering external trough coloured rendering services to domestic and commercial customers throughout the North West of England.

We are approved external render applicators for many of the renders systems manufacturers as ( PAREX , K-REND, WEBER, WETHERBY, FASSA BORTOLO ) and regularly work closely with them to deliver high quality projects. We use a wide range of renders systems that are the best on the market and the render used come with guarantees as well as a manufacturers guarantee.

The external monocouche render are waterproof, throughout coloured, modern and more flexible than traditional sand and cement, making them more suitable for the British weather, and they are available in many colours and modern finish types as: Scratch Render, Roughcast Render and Wet dash Render.

Our external rendering team only uses a machine rendering pump which provide reductions in labour and scaffold time plus faster completion mean that JK Rendering Systems offer real cost benefits over traditional methods and our renderers are fully qualified, experienced and CSCS registered to deliver high quality standard of work.

We are proud to say that we have a great team and we love to take pride in all our work. No job is too big or too small. OUR SERVICES

As well as providing the best service possible, we also offer the following…

MONOCOUCHE

Colour scratch Render (Monocouche) – colour render is a high performance coloured render based exterior wall coating system. The colour is contained in a special pigment, which is mixed into the render. When it is applied to the outside of a building the wall surface is a colour of your choosing which requires no painting at all; It’s completely waterproof and will not fade. It adds years of life to your property providing a new wall finish. It can also add value to your house.

ROUGHCAST RENDER

Roughcast render is a decorative exterior masonry coating applied to walls using a wet mortar and produces a softer finished texture.

EXTERNAL WALL INSULATION

EWI involves external cladding the building with insulation, some houses can let out heat, making your home inefficient and expensive. EWI is fixed onto your outside wall, then applying a base coat and mesh, then a decorative finish is rendered onto the top.
Reduced draughts – Covers and fills holes and cracks in the brickwork.
Weather proofing – which helps withstand the most extreme weather conditions.
Increasing sound resistance.
Saves money – can reduce your heating bills.

BRICK EFFECT

Brick effect render is a versatile two coat specialist render that is similar to the appearance of bricks. Brick effect renders can be used with external wall insulation and specialist rendering systems.

LIME RENDER

Lime can be used in place of cement render where flexibility and breathability is needed. It is traditionally applied to stone or brick buildings.

SOFT WASH

Colour render and other varieties of render are ideally suited to cleaning with soft washing.

EXTERNAL WALL INSULATION

JK Rendering Systems are number one when it comes to External Wall Insulation.

THE BENEFITS

An un-insulated solid wall home will lose around 35-45% of the heat through its walls and it’s highly likely that if your home was built before 1920 then the external walls will be solid. This is a huge amount of heat loss. The good news is that by combating this through insulating your solid walls can significantly reduce heat loss and could save you around £400 to £500 a year on your fuel bills. EWI which is short of External or ‘Solid’ Wall Insulation is a form of insulation that is fixed to the exterior of a property. This is then rendered to give both a new decorative and a protective to the existing facade.

There are many benefits to having an External Wall Insulation System.
You will save money on your heating bills
By installing our EWI system, insulating your external walls, you can significantly reduce heat loss and cut your heating bills.
The comfort levels within the house will improve
No more keeping the heating on as your home will warm up more quickly and maintain a comfortable temperature.
The external appearance of the property will be improved
With the installation of EWI, your home will be transformed with a wide range of contemporary or traditional finishes with a choice of textures, colours and finished effects. The high performance render finishes used on the Fassa system offer excellent durability and protection. Our finishes have the colour pigments added during the mixing process which means they never have to be painted, providing you with a low maintenance home.
Your property value will have increased
The value of your home could increase with a transformation in its appearance and increased thermal performance the value of your home could increase.
